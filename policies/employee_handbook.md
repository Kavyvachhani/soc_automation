# Employee Handbook
**Attest Corporation — Effective: 2026-01-01 | Version: 3.2**

---

## 1. Welcome

Welcome to Attest Corporation. This handbook sets out the policies, expectations, and
resources that apply to all employees. It is not a contract of employment; the Company
reserves the right to amend these policies at any time with reasonable notice.

---

## 2. Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to:

- Act with integrity and honesty in all professional dealings.
- Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect and dignity.
- Avoid conflicts of interest and disclose any potential conflicts promptly.
- Protect Company assets, data, and intellectual property.
- Comply with all applicable laws and regulations.

---

## 3. Work Hours & Remote Work

- Core hours are 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM local time, Monday through Friday.
- Flexible scheduling is available with manager approval.
- Remote work is permitted for eligible roles; see the Remote Work Policy addendum.
- Overtime must be pre-approved by your manager and HR.

---

## 4. Data Privacy & Confidentiality

Employees who handle personal data must:

- Access only data necessary for their role (principle of least privilege).
- Never share credentials, access tokens, or passwords.
- Report any suspected data breach immediately to security@attest.io.
- Complete annual data-protection training.
- Adhere to the Non-Disclosure Agreement signed at onboarding.

---

## 5. Information Security

- Use only Company-approved devices and software.
- Lock your screen when stepping away from your workstation.
- Enable full-disk encryption and screen lock on all devices.
- Connect to the VPN when accessing internal systems remotely.
- Report lost or stolen devices to IT within one hour.

---

## 6. Acceptable Use of Technology

See the separate Acceptable Use Policy. Key points:

- Company systems are for business use; incidental personal use is tolerated.
- Never install unlicensed software.
- Do not access, store, or transmit illegal, obscene, or harassing content.

---

## 7. Leave & Time Off

- Annual leave: 20 days per year (pro-rated for partial years).
- Sick leave: 10 days per year; doctor's note required after 3 consecutive days.
- Public holidays: as per the local statutory calendar.
- Parental leave: 16 weeks primary caregiver, 6 weeks secondary caregiver.

---

## 8. Performance & Development

- Employees receive a formal performance review every six months.
- Each employee will have a personal development plan reviewed quarterly.
- Training budget: USD 2,000 per employee per year.

---

## 9. Disciplinary Procedure

Violations of Company policy may result in disciplinary action up to and including
termination. Serious violations (e.g., data breaches, harassment, fraud) may result in
immediate dismissal without prior warning.

---

## 10. Reporting Concerns

- Anonymous reporting: ethics@attest.io or the HR hotline.
- The Company prohibits retaliation against any employee who reports a concern in good faith.

---

*Employee acknowledgement of this handbook is captured during the onboarding workflow.*
